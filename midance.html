Value Proposition Statement


Mi Dance Mirror & Video project is an impressive IOT product which helps dance enthusiasts learn about dance programs from videos in a more convenient and effective way.
It includes:
1.Online dance video-sharing app, which can help users search and enjoy dance online videos.
2. Smart mirror, which can connect the app and display videos, show virtual stage effects and reflects user's figures.
3. Smart camera, which can record user's performance.

Problems & Needs

  • The market is lacking of professional dance video sharing website.
  • Videos always mirror anti-directional action, so that users couldn't imitate movements easily.
  • Dancers couldn't check both video device and mirror at the same time. Because video screen is regarded as too small, when dancers make a distance from the screen by performing movement, it's hard to check the screen.
  • Users want voice control the video app.
  • Users want to take the video of their performance, but the smartphone couldn't play video and set camera at the same time.
  • Users want a virtual stage at home or studio.

Solutions

  • Users can search and watch dance program videos through MiDance video-sharing App.
  • Mi smart mirror can connect to Mi dance app, and display videos. Therefore, the smart mirror can not only reflect the user's movement, but also showing the dance video.
  • Users can use voice to control smart mirror device.
  • Smart mirror with a camera can take user's performance video, with immersive stage and lighting effects.

Features

1. Search a wanted dance video from Mi App.

2. Play the video in Mi App or Mi dance mirror.

3. Connect the Mi dance mirror with Mi App.

4.Voice control the Mi dance mirror.
