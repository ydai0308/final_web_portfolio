UX/UI, Mobile,Smart home

Value Proposition Statement

Valentia is an Internet of Things (IOT) product, which combining with the Valentia App and the smart wardrobe. Valentia can help users to decide what to wear everyday, which depends on the weather, occasion, and clothing style of the user's set up. Furthermore,User can easily organize and set their wardrobe through Valentia App.

Problems & Needs

1.It’s not easy to decide what to wear and pair up good looking outfits when time is pressing.
2. Although some girls have a great deal of garments, but they aways forget which items they already purchased.
3. Women sometimes prefer the comfortable and simpler outfits to wear, due to the shortage of time, rather than considering the occasion and whether it’s flattering.
4.Women need a smart thing which can automatically help them select the right clothes, for every situation.
