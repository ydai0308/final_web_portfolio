Archi.VR Project

AAU-2016-Fall-WNM643-Interactive Design & Concepts
Instructor: Changying Zheng
UX/UI Project

Problems & Needs

The students who are learning architecture design and young architects need to learn design skills from great architects. They want to travel to well-known cities and visit masterpiece architectures to broaden their horizons. Unfortunately, they don't have enough time and money for travel. Therefore, they need a Virtual Reality (VR) product, which has the masterpiece architectures' informations to help them study and research architectures in a more comprehensive and visualized way. They want to check the inside and outside appearances, environment, and blueprints of the architecture.

Project Introduction

Archi.VR is an App combining with the virtual reality(VR)device that shows the visions and databases of San Francisco’s significant architectures in the VR model. Archi.VR through the use of Virtual Reality technology, shows the buildings’ inside and outside appearances. Users can simulate walking around the building then have immersive experiences. It also shows the architecture's environment from the city's panorama. More over, users can check various versions of the structure's blueprints and detailed informations.
